Biking in Stockholm

Guided Bike Tours

Stockholm is ranked ad The Most Sustainable Country in the World for many years and cycling around Stockholm is considered as one of the easiest and smartest way to discover the city. In the city there are many cycling lanes since people living in Stockholm are used to use bikes for moving.
During summertime there are many organized bike tours that will allow you to see the most breathtaking sightseeing of the city. You can find tours for both city centre as Gamla Stan or the nature around the city, such as the islands.
In the first case the tour will be mostly concentrated on the historical buildings and streets: the guide will get you through explanations about the old town, one of the best and well preserved medieval city; the 18th-century Stockholm Palace, official residence of the Swedish monarch; the Swedish Parliament and more.
Moreover there are tours for specific neighborhoods like Södermalm, known as the hippiest district of the city: there you can admire the typical architecture, the many art galleries and the cozy atmosphere of the coffee shops.
Around the city there are many islands, highly preserved, with the real Swedish nature. One great example is the island of Djurgården where you can explore the amazing landscapes and the typical house architecture riding on two wheels. The guide will explain about the history of this island and its fauna and flora evolution.
Finally one last possibility if you are a row nature's lover are the mountain bike tours: this is the way you can experience the Swedish woods and the funniest trails close to the city centre. One of the most famous is the tour in Nackareservatet national forest: there the guide will safety bring you into the wood where you can see the pristine scenery.
