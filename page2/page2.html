Biking in Stockholm

Renting Bikes

The choice of renting a bike has many advantages: a free assistance service is guaranteed whenever you need, even in case of puncture, than a low deductible is applied in case of theft, and you can change the type of bike when you want, e.g. from a city bike to a mountain bike or a E-bike.
In Stockholm there are many places where you can easily rent a bike:
  • Stockholm eBike: this is the Stockholm’s rental bike system. There are more that 100 spots around the city where you can pick up your electric bike, and than return it wherever you want. You can easily manage everything through the App, and the prices are very convenient.
  • Jonna bike: this service has really good reviews; they have two main stores: Långholmsgatan 30 (Hornstull) and Albanovägen 19 (Albano). They guarantee theft protection and a special service of delivery and assistance at home.
  • Rent a bike: this rental service is well known in Sweden and offers different types of bikes for all the needs. You can check for bike availability online and than you can pick it up in the store that have in Kungsbro strand 21 (Kungsholmen) in the city centre, or in Flädermorsbacken 22 (Bromma) in the western Stockholm.
  • Gamla Stans Cykel: this is the oldest bicycle shop in the city; there you can feel the tradition of bikes in Stockholm, handed down through the generations since 1913. The store is located in the Old Town and it is both renting and selling bicycles and accessories.
  • Cykelstallet: this rental service is in Scheelegatan 15 (Kungsholmen), in the city center, and offers a huge variety of bikes from the electric or hybrid bikes to the racing or mountain bikes, and also tandem bikes! The assistance service is always available and the helmets are included in the rental.
