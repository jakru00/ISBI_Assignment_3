Biking in Stockholm

Buying a bike in Stockholm

Sweden's capital is a great place for biking and if you don't already have one, here's where to look. Stockholm has many bycicle shops which offer a variety of quality bike brands. The most popular bike shops are listed below. However, the second-hand market is big in Stockholm and bikes are no exception. They can be found in bike retailers, second-hand websites as well as private listings on Facebook etc. This page shows you where and how to look for pre-loved bikes as well as new ones.
Guide to buying a new bike in Stockholm
The most well reviewed bike shops in Stockholm include:
  • Jarla Cycle & Sport - two locations in Stockholm, bike shop as well as service station
  • Söders Cykelbutik i Stockholm - located on Södermalm, browse their collection in the web shop as well
  • Urban Bike Wear - they specialise in biking wear but also offer bikes and frames
Second-hand bikes in Stockholm
The second-hand market in Stockholm is great - you can find almost anything pre-loved. There is different possibilities to purchase a used bike: second-hand bike shops, websites and private sellers. Some bike stores in Stockholm also buy and sell used bikes like:
  • Second hand bike in Östermalm
  • Tonnys cyklar
  • irecycle - this shop has a website where you can see all available bikes and a physical store as well
To search the whole country you can also look for pre-used bikes on the internet. There are several websites for second-hand purchases which are popular in Sweden - the biggest ones are blocket and tradera. There you can buy bikes from private sellers either at a fixed price or in an auction.
