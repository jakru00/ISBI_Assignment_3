Biking in Stockholm

Bike Equipment

When riding a bike is very important to have the correct equipment; right tools can make you biking experience more safe and easy.
  • Helmet: this is the first tool you will need; it’s very important to wear it in order to prevent serious injuries in case of falling. There are many types of helmets for all the sizes, and it’s very easy to find a light one, that can also help for sun or rain.
  • Gloves: whoever has ever ridden a bike in Stockholm will confirm that you definitely need the gloves. Also in this case you can choose the one that best fits your need, basing on the weather. In wintertime you should use some warm and waterproof gloves.
  • Light: according with the statistics the first reason of accidents with a bike involved is the lack of lights on the bike itself. It’s crucial to have a light on your bike in order to avoid accident and be able to see properly.
  • Locker: if you are using your bike in the city center you will definitely need a locker; unfortunately the bike theft is still very common in Stockholm, and a good locker is the only way of trying to prevent it. There are many types of lockers from standard metal chain to the U-lock that is now very used, the important thing is to chose a strong one.
  • Basket: this is a useful tool you might want to put your bags and shoppers during your riding; it is also very useful for containing the locker.
  • Saddle cover: this tool can make your ride way more conferrable, allowing you to use your bike more frequently and for longer distances. Also in this case there are many types of covers: since in Stockholm, especially in the Old Town, the pavement is made by bricks, you should consider to take a soft saddle cover, that can cushion.
