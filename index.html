Biking in Stockholm

Enjoy Stockholm by bike

Biking is one of the easiest ways to get around Stockholm. Besides being environmentally friendly, it is often times faster and more flexible than public transport. The infrastructure for bycicles in Stockholm is fantastic - whether in the city centre or in the suburbs there are almost always wide bike paths separated from cars and pedestrians. In addition there are dedicated parking spaces and bike stands almost everywhere across town. Even air pump stations can be found in multiple places so you can refill your tires for free to enjoy biking even more. As you see biking is the perfect way to exercise as well as explore Stockholm's beautiful capital all at once.



This page shows you everything you need to know about biking in Stockholm including where to rent or buy a bike, the most beautiful bike trails around Stockholm and tourist-friendly biking activities. If you are on holiday in Stockholm and are looking for a way to see the town without worrying where to go, a guided tour on bikes might be the best for you! Also check out our guide to rental bikes if you want a bit more flexibility.
If you are here for longer, we would recommend buying a bike - that way you can explore the city on your own schedule. Stockholms offers qualitiy bikes as well as plenty of equipment , too. To find out about the most popular areas for biking around Stockholm, see our page on bike trails.
